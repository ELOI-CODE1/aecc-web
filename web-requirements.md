# AAEC Ltd Website Requirements and Design Guide

## 1. Project Overview
AAEC Ltd is an architecture, engineering, and construction consulting firm based in Kigali, Rwanda. The website should present the company as professional, innovative, and reliable while making it easy for clients to contact the company.

## 2. Main Objectives
- Introduce the company and its services
- Build trust and credibility
- Show expertise in architecture, engineering, and construction consulting
- Encourage inquiries through phone, email, or a contact form
- Highlight the company’s values, experience, and project workflow

## 3. Target Audience
- Private homeowners
- Commercial and industrial clients
- Government and institutional organizations
- NGOs and development partners
- Contractors, consultants, and project stakeholders

## 4. Recommended Website Structure
The website should be a modern, professional, responsive website with the following pages:

1. Home
   - Hero section
   - Company introduction
   - Key services
   - Why choose AAEC
   - Call-to-action buttons

2. About Us
   - Company profile
   - Vision, mission, and values
   - History and experience

3. Services
   - Architectural Design
   - Structural Engineering
   - Civil Engineering
   - Quantity Surveying
   - Construction Supervision
   - Project Management
   - Environmental Consulting
   - GIS & Surveying

4. Portfolio / Projects
   - Featured projects
   - Project categories by industry
   - Photos or project summaries

5. Industries Served
   - Residential
   - Commercial
   - Industrial
   - Government
   - Education
   - Healthcare
   - Hospitality
   - NGOs

6. Contact Us
   - Phone numbers
   - Email address
   - Physical address
   - Contact form
   - Map or location section

## 5. Functional Requirements
The website should include:
- Responsive design for mobile, tablet, and desktop
- Clear navigation menu
- Contact form with name, email, phone, message, and service interest
- Clickable phone numbers
- Social media links (if available)
- Fast-loading pages
- SEO-friendly titles and meta descriptions
- Clean image gallery for projects and team work
- Call-to-action buttons such as “Request a Consultation”

## 6. Content Requirements
The website should use the following core content:
- Company name: AAEC Ltd – Apex Architecture & Engineering Consulting Ltd
- Tagline: Designing Solutions, Building Futures
- Company description: Architecture, Engineering & Construction Consulting firm based in Kigali, Rwanda
- Vision, mission, and core values
- Services list
- Why choose AAEC section
- Industries served
- Project workflow
- Software and technology used
- HSE commitment
- Contact information

## 7. Design Recommendations
### Visual Style
The design should feel:
- Professional
- Modern
- Trustworthy
- Engineering-focused
- Elegant and clean

### Color Palette
Suggested colors:
- Deep navy blue for professionalism and trust
- Gold or amber for highlights and accents
- White for clean background space
- Light gray for subtle section separation

### Typography
- Use modern sans-serif fonts such as Inter, Poppins, or Montserrat
- Headings should be bold and clear
- Body text should be easy to read and spacious

### Layout Style
Recommended layout structure:
- Sticky top navigation bar
- Hero section with strong headline and background image
- Service cards in a grid layout
- About section with image and short company story
- Project showcase section
- Testimonials or trust indicators (optional)
- Contact section with clear CTA
- Footer with company details and quick links

## 8. Suggested Design Sections
### Hero Section
Include:
- Main headline: “Designing Solutions, Building Futures”
- Short supporting text about AAEC Ltd
- Primary button: “Get in Touch”
- Secondary button: “Explore Services”
- Professional architecture or construction background image

### About Section
Show:
- Company profile
- Vision and mission
- Core values
- A professional image of the company or team

### Services Section
Display services as cards with icons and short descriptions.

### Why Choose AAEC Section
Highlight:
- Experienced professionals
- Quality assurance
- Modern technology
- Cost-effective solutions
- Timely delivery

### Contact Section
Include:
- Phone numbers
- Contact form
- Office location
- Map embed if available

## 9. Recommended Technology Stack
For a professional website, the following options are suitable:
- Simple static website: HTML, CSS, and JavaScript
- Modern approach: React or Next.js with Tailwind CSS
- For easier content updates: WordPress or a CMS

For this project, a modern responsive website using HTML/CSS/JavaScript or Next.js would be a strong choice.

## 10. SEO and Performance Guidelines
- Add meaningful page titles and meta descriptions
- Use optimized images with alt text
- Keep the website lightweight for fast loading
- Make sure the website is mobile-friendly
- Use clear headings and structured content

## 11. Final Design Direction
The website should present AAEC Ltd as a leading consulting firm in Rwanda with a polished, professional appearance that reflects quality, innovation, and reliability. The user experience should be simple, clear, and focused on converting visitors into clients.
